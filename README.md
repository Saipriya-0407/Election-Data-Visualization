Election Data Visualization

Milestone-1: State Analysis
  
  --Shows the vote share and winning party distribution across different states, helping identify strongholds of each party.

  --Highlights regional voting patterns and trends, such as which states favor certain political parties consistently.

  --Helps compare state-wise performance over elections, making it easier to spot changes in voter behavior.

Milestone-2: Constituency Analysis
  
  --Displays winning candidates and parties at the constituency level, giving a detailed local view of election results.

  --Helps identify close contests and vote margin differences between candidates in each constituency.

  --Shows voter preferences and trends within constituencies, useful for understanding micro-level political behavior.

Milestone-3: Party Performance Analysis
  
  --Shows the overall seats won and vote share of each political party, helping compare their performance.

  --Highlights top-performing and underperforming parties across different regions or elections.

  --Helps analyze trends in party growth or decline over time, indicating shifts in public support.

Milestone-4: Overview Analysis
  
  --Provides a summary of total seats, vote share, and overall winning party, giving a quick snapshot of the election outcome.

  --Combines insights from state, constituency, and party levels to present a complete picture of the results.

  --Helps identify major trends and patterns, such as dominant parties and overall voter behavior across the election.
